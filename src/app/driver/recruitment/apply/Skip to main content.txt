# How subscriptions work

Learn how Preply subscriptions work, including payments, scheduling, flexibility, and how to manage your plan.

## What is a subscription?
A subscription on Preply is a plan where you choose how many lessons you’d like to take each week. Lessons are paid for automatically every 28 days, so your learning continues without interruptions. You can schedule lessons for any future date, not just within the current 28-day period.

## Why are subscriptions helpful?
Learning regularly builds habits and keeps motivation high. Subscriptions are designed to make that consistency easier.
For students:
- Stay motivated – regular lessons keep your progress steady. 
- Build momentum – committing to lessons helps you practice more.​
- See better results – learning consistently leads to stronger outcomes.​

## How subscriptions work

- First payment: When you start a subscription, you pay for your first 28 days of lessons.​
- Automatic renewal: Every 28 days, your plan will renew. Preply will charge your saved payment method and add new lessons to your balance for the next 28 days.​
- Flexibility: You can pause, cancel, or adjust your subscription at any time.

## Scheduling and rescheduling lessons
With a subscription, you can schedule weekly lessons for a set time each week or book one by one on any future date your tutor is available (not just within the current payment period)
Once your lessons are scheduled, you can see your full schedule on the My lessons page. From there, you can manage your lessons by rescheduling or canceling them. For step-by-step guidance, see our article on [scheduling, rescheduling, and cancellation](https://help.preply.com/en/articles/4171360-student-s-lesson-management-scheduling-rescheduling-cancellation).

### How can I pay for a single (individual) lesson instead of buying the subscription?
It’s not possible to make one-time payments for individual lessons on Preply. However, subscription plans are flexible and you can adjust or pause them to organize your learning in the way that works best for you.

### What’s the minimum subscription plan?
The minimum plan you can choose is 1 lesson per week. Because subscriptions run on a 4-week cycle (28 days), this means:
- You pay for 4 lessons upfront when you subscribe.
- After that, you’ll be charged every 4 weeks for the same number of lessons (4 lessons/month).
You pay for 4 lessons upfront when you subscribe.

### Do I have to schedule the same number of lessons each week with a subscription?
No. Once your lessons are added to your balance, you can schedule them at any time that works for you — even beyond the current subscription cycle. We recommend scheduling weekly lessons for the best progress, but the pace is completely up to you.

### Can I have more than one tutor?
Yes. You can have a separate subscription with each tutor you want to learn with.

### Can I change my tutor for an existing subscription?
Yes. You can transfer your subscription balance to a new tutor. See our guide on[how to transfer funds between tutors](https://help.preply.com/en/articles/7924262-transfer-of-funds-among-tutors) for step-by-step instructions.

### What happens if I can’t schedule all my lessons before the next billing date?
Your lessons won’t expire as long as they’re scheduled on your tutor’s calendar — even if the lesson is scheduled for a date in your next billing cycle. You can always reschedule later if needed.
